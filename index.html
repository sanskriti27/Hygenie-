About
Hygenie üßº

Introducing our revolutionary app built at Google Solution Challenge'23 that makes finding clean and safe public restrooms easier than ever before! With features like multilingual support, privacy protection, and emergency assistance, our app is the ultimate companion for all your restroom needs. It even provides tips on sustainable restroom usage and lets users contribute photos and report issues. Our app also offers real-time updates on availability and cleanliness, along with information on amenities and accessibility for people with disabilities. Plus, with location-based offers and event-based queue length indicators, our app ensures a comfortable and hassle-free restroom experience.

Features

Amenities Information

Provides information on the availability of amenities like soap, hand sanitizer, and toilet seat covers.

Accessibility Information

Provides information on the accessibility of restrooms for people with disabilities.

Emergency Assistance

In case of emergency, users can easily connect with nearby emergency services.

User-Contributed Photos

Users contributed photos to help others assess the cleanliness and safety of restrooms.

Real-time Updates

Provides real-time updates on the availability and cleanliness of nearby restrooms.

Queue Length Indicator

Indicates the length of the queue at concerts and other events to help users plan their restroom visit.

Multilingual Support

Supports multiple languages, making it accessible to users worldwide.

Baby Changing Facility Indicator

Indicates the availability of baby changing facilities in restrooms.

Eco-friendly Toilet Indicator

Indicates the availability of eco-friendly toilet facilities.
